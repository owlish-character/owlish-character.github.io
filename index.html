Choosing the right major is always an important step when researching how to become a quality control engineer.

When we researched the most common majors for a quality control engineer, we found that they most commonly earn bachelor's degree or master's degree.

Other degrees that we often see on quality control engineer resumes include associate degree degrees or diploma degrees.

You may find that experience in other jobs will help you become a quality control engineer.

In fact, many quality control engineer jobs require experience in a role such as quality assurance engineer.

Meanwhile, many quality control engineers also have previous career experience in roles such as project engineer or quality control inspector.
